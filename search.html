Interesting facts about aloe

Aloe is a cool plant

  • Aloe can survive for over 100 years
  • Aloe is not a cactus, but its own species of plant
  • There are over 500 varieties of aloe plants
  • Aloe is 95% water, 5% beneficial solids
  • The aloe plant has self-healing properties
  • Aloe juice is a key ingredient in healthy moothies
